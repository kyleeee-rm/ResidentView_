PROCEDURE

01 Fill Form

Complete the job seeker certificate application form with accurate information.

02 Submit Documents

Prepare and bring required documents to the Barangay Hall.

03 Verification

Barangay staff will verify your eligibility and supporting documents.

04 Get Certificate

Receive your First-Time Job Seeker Certificate for official use.

Please collect all the necessary requirements before applying then provide the required information.

Your registration details will be used to complete the basic information required for the application. If you wish to update or make changes to your information, please proceed to the Barangay Hall for assistance.

Statement of Truth and Affirmation

⚠️ WARNING: Penalties for Misdeclaration
  • Criminal prosecution under the Revised Penal Code
  • Civil penalties and surcharges
  • Cancellation of the issued certificate
  • Additional taxes and interest on unpaid amounts
  • Legal action for tax evasion

Required Documents to Bring:

Please bring the following documents to the Barangay Hall for verification and processing.

  • Valid Government-issued ID (Original and Photocopy)
  • Birth Certificate (Original and Photocopy)
  • Diploma or Certificate of Graduation (Original and Photocopy)
  • Transcript of Records (if college graduate or undergraduate)
  • Barangay Clearance
  • 2 pieces of 2x2 ID Pictures
  • Completed Application Form

Note: Processing fee may apply. Please inquire at the Barangay Hall for current rates.
